What is an iot Applications?
IoT application is the interface connecting hardware along with the provision of various features connected through the means of internet.

What are the main Features needed?
Amazon Alexa App,Spotify Connect, Surveillance Apps, CCTV Cameras

What are some Examples?
Seamless Connection, Security, Accessibility, Friendly UI,

Activity 2: Smart Building

What does smart mean?
Ability to solve a complex problem in a simpler manner
Ability to monitor, ‘process’ user data and act accordingly (a.k.a Sense-Think-Act Paradigm)

what are some building scenario?
Old Age Homes, Traffic Light Cameras,Pet Shelter, Water System leak Detection, Baby Care systems, High level security

what are the main features needed?
Feasible, Economically viable, Central control on data and resources, Energy efficient, Telecommunications(Low bandwidth)
